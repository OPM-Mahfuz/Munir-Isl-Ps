About Munir Islamic Primary School

Munir Islamic Primary School is dedicated to providing quality Islamic education rooted in Islamic values, combined with modern foundational skills. Our goal is to nurture well-rounded students who excel academically and morally in a caring environment.

Our Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive curriculum that integrates Islamic studies with core subjects such as Mathematics, Science, Languages, and Social Studies. Emphasis is placed on moral development, character building, and community service.

Core Subjects:

  • Quran and Islamic Studies
  • Arabic Language
  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
